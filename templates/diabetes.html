Diabetes Prediction

The following features are used in diabetes prediction models:

Description

The following features are used in diabetes prediction models:

  • Pregnancies: Number of times a woman has been pregnant

  • Glucose: Plasma glucose concentration (mg/dL) from an oral glucose tolerance test

  • Blood Pressure: Diastolic blood pressure (mm Hg)

  • Skin Thickness: Triceps skinfold thickness (mm), used to estimate body fat

  • Insulin: 2-hour serum insulin (μU/ml), an indicator of pancreatic function

  • BMI: Body Mass Index (BMI), a measure of body fat (weight in kg / height in m²)

  • Diabetes Pedigree Function: A function that scores the likelihood of diabetes based on family history

  • Age: Age of the individual in years

  • Diabetes: 1 = Present, 0 = Absent
