Heart Disease Prediction

Description

  • Gender (M/F): Male or Female

  • Chest Pain Type: ATA, NAP, ASY, ST(ATA=Type0,NAP=Type1,ASY=Type2,ST=Type3)

  • Resting Blood Pressure: Measured in mm Hg

  • Cholesterol Level: Serum cholesterol in mg/dL

  • Fasting Blood Sugar: 1 if > 120 mg/dL, else 0

  • Resting ECG: Normal, ST, or other abnormalities(Normal=Type0,ST=Type=1,other=Type2)

  • Max Heart Rate: Maximum heart rate achieved

  • Exercise-Induced Angina: Y = Yes, N = No

  • ST Depression: Depression during exercise

  • Slope of ST Segment: Up, Flat, Down

  • Heart Disease: 1 = Present, 0 = Absent
